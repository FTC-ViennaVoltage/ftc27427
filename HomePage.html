Who we are

Built by students.
Driven by curiosity.

Vienna Voltage (FTC 27427) is a community-organized robotics team dedicated to promoting STEM education and inspiring students throughout our community.

We're a group of high school students who believe the excitement of building and competing with robots can light a spark in younger learners — one that lasts a lifetime.
